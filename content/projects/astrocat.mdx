Astrocat is a real time messenging app that allows users to chat with each other. Users can also create group chats with three or more people. Users can search other users that they wish to chat with and see the online/offline status as well. Users can also send photos and files. The website also supports dark and light mode. 

The app also has a built in cat bot called 'Astro' that users can chat with. Astro is a friendly, adventurous space cat AI who is always there to brighten your day. Astro can chat with you, tell you jokes, give you advice and answer any questions you have.
